Devalgarh

Devalgarh hosts the remnants of an ancient fort, serving as a silent testament to its glorious past. The town's name itself resonates with its past. “Deval” translates to “god,” and “garh” signifies “fort.” Devalgarh, therefore, translates to “Fort of the Gods,” a fitting title for a place steeped in religious and historical importance. It is situated 18-19 kilometers from Srinagar Garhwal on the Khirsu-Srinagar road.
