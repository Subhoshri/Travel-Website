About Khirsu

Khirsu is an offbeat destination in Pauri Garhwal district of Uttarakhand, India. It is renowned for its captivating beauty that's popular with nature lovers and budget travelers, and is known for its tranquility and isolation. For a rejuvenating vacation, this place is ideal as it offers panoramic views of the majestic Himalayan peaks, including Trishul, Nanda Devi, and Panchachuli.
